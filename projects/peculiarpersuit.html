Role: Editor, sound designer, VFX artist and motion designer

    Team: Caylen Burger, Hanna Jones, Sophie Martin, Kayla Taifalos

    Tools: Adobe After Effects

    Summary of Project

    For this project, we were tasked to complete a production bible and animatic for a chosen story adaptation from an Edward Lear poem. Our group of 4 consisted of team members:

    • Caylen Burger (Producer, scriptwriter, graphic designer, cinematographer)
    • Hanna Jones (Director, line artist, storyboard artist, colour artist, concept artist, scriptwriter, storyboard artist)
    • myself, Sophie Martin (Editor, sound designer, VFX artist, motion designer)
    • and Kayla Taifalos (Line artist, colour artist, concept artist, storyboard artist)

    My role

    • Drafted a rough animatic using the storyboards in Adobe After Effects
    • Noted and sourced the necessary sound effects and copyright-free audio
    • Created the final animatic in Adobe After Effects
    • Adjusted scenes and shots based on feedback
